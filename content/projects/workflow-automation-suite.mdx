## Overview

Workflow Automation Suite helps cross-functional teams automate repetitive business processes with a visual pipeline builder, trigger system, and rules engine.

## What I Built

I led frontend architecture, introduced a scalable design system, and implemented optimistic updates for high-frequency interactions. This kept the interface responsive even with large datasets.

## Impact

The product reduced manual operations overhead and became the core interface used daily by internal teams.
