## Overview

Transcript Automation App was designed to eliminate repetitive manual transcript entry from structured and semi-structured PDF reports. I created a processing pipeline that reads source documents, extracts required data points, validates fields, and formats consistent outputs for downstream use.

## Tech Stack

- C#
- .NET
- PDF Parsing

## Outcome

The app significantly reduced turnaround time for transcript preparation while improving consistency across records. The workflow also lowered human error rates by enforcing parsing rules and clear validation steps before export.
